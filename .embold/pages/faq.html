Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions.

What is this app used for?

This app helps you manage tasks, track progress, and stay organized with real‑time updates.

How do I create a new account?

You will receive an invitation email. Click the link, set your password, and your account activates automatically.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings and that the app has permission to send alerts.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. You can enter data offline, and it will sync automatically once your device reconnects to the internet.

How do I report an issue?

Use the Contact page on this website or send a message through the in‑app support form.

Is my data secure?

All data is encrypted and stored securely. Only authorized users can access your information.
