Popovers

Popovers

Popovers offer additional information or interaction options. They feature a triangular arrow pointing to the trigger.

1. Directional Variants

Top Popover
Points downwards to the trigger element.
Bottom Popover
Points upwards to the trigger element.
Left Popover
Points right to the trigger.
Right Popover
Points left to the trigger.

2. Complex Content

Popovers can contain text, images, videos, and actions.
